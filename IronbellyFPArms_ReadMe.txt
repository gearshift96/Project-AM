OPEN PROJECT
Open the .uproject file in the Unreal engine. You should have version 4.10 or higher installed.

ANIMATIONS
To demo the animations, click the Play button in Unreal.
Punch - Left Click
Push Door - Right Click
Sprint - Hold W + Shift
Jump - Space
Grab Low - G
Grab High - H
Throw - T

CHANGE FP ARM
1) In your Content Browser, go to Content > FirstPersonBPIronbelly > Blueprints
2) Open the FirstPersonCharacterIronbelly Blueprint
3) Click on the Viewport tab
4) Click on the FP arm mesh, these will be in idle animation mode
5) On the right hand side of your screen, go to the Details tab > Mesh and select a new Skeletal Mesh from the dropdown
6) Close the Blueprint
7) Click the play button to view newly selected FP arms
